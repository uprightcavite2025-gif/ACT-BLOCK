Trust Driver Connection

The chosen intervention is linked to a primary and, in some cases, a secondary trust driver. This connection helps you understand what condition of trust the intervention is mainly designed to strengthen.

The primary trust driver shows where the intervention is most directly focused. A secondary trust driver shows where additional trust effects may also appear. This gives more clarity, predictability, and confidence before you prepare the intervention.

Apply the Intervention

You are now ready to conduct the intervention in your working environment.

Advice for this week

Use your preparation as a guide over the next one week. The purpose is not to deliver a perfect session, but to apply the intervention intentionally and observe how trust responds in real work conditions.

Focus on clear communication, respectful dialogue, and the trust signals you selected. Give yourself enough time to notice how people respond before deciding what changed.
